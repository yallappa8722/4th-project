Local Tips

Food

Try the local cuisine, including baguettes, croissants, and escargots. Visit small cafes for an authentic experience.

Transportation

Use the metro for quick and affordable travel around the city. Walking is also a great way to explore!

Culture

Learn basic phrases in the local language to show respect. Always greet with "Bonjour" and say "Merci" when leaving.
